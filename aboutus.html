About Apollo Hospital

For over four decades, Apollo Hospitals has been at the forefront of medical excellence, providing world-class healthcare services with compassion and innovation.

Our Story

From a single hospital to Asia's largest healthcare network

Pioneering Healthcare Since 1983

Apollo Hospitals was founded with the vision of bringing world-class healthcare to India. What started as a 150-bed hospital has now grown into one of the most respected healthcare networks in Asia.

Our journey has been marked by numerous medical milestones, including performing India's first successful liver transplant and pioneering coronary artery bypass surgery.

Today, Apollo Hospitals operates over 70 hospitals with more than 10,000 beds across India and internationally, serving millions of patients each year.
